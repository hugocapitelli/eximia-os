# KB_04: Major Biblical Themes Reference

## Introduction

This knowledge base indexes major biblical themes with key passages, theological development, and modern application. Themes are cross-referenced for study and spiritual understanding.

---

## FAITH & TRUST (Fé e Confiança)

### Definition
Trust in God's character, promises, and ultimate provision despite circumstances or uncertainty.

### Key Passages
- **Hebrews 11:1** — "Now faith is the assurance of things hoped for, the conviction of things not seen"
- **Romans 3:28** — "We hold that one is justified by faith apart from works of the law"
- **John 3:16** — Belief/faith in Christ leads to eternal life
- **Proverbs 3:5-6** — Trust in Lord with all heart
- **Psalm 37:3-5** — Trust and do good

### Theological Development
- **OT**: Trust in God's promises to Abraham, deliverance from Egypt, protection in wilderness
- **Gospels**: Jesus emphasizes faith (Mark 11:24, Matthew 14:31)
- **Paul**: Faith apart from works justifies (Romans 3-4)
- **Hebrews**: Faith endurance through perseverance (Hebrews 11)
- **James**: Faith without works is dead (James 2:26)

### Related Themes
- Hope & Redemption
- Providence & God's Care
- Surrender & Obedience

### Modern Application
- Trusting God in financial uncertainty
- Faith during health crisis
- Believing God's promises when circumstances contradict
- Moving forward without guarantees

### Study Questions
1. What specific circumstance am I struggling to trust God with?
2. How did biblical figures demonstrate faith in impossible situations?
3. What does faith look like practically (not just emotionally)?

## HOPE & REDEMPTION (Esperança & Redenção)

### Definition
Confident expectation of God's redemptive work; confidence in Christ's resurrection and renewal of all things.

### Key Passages
- **Romans 8:24-25** — Hope is for what we do not yet see
- **1 Peter 1:3-4** — Living hope through Christ's resurrection
- **Colossians 1:27** — "Christ in you, the hope of glory"
- **Psalm 42:5** — Why are you downcast? Hope in God
- **Titus 2:13** — "Awaiting our blessed hope, the appearing of our God"

### Theological Development
- **OT**: Hope in God's deliverance, restoration, and promised Messiah
- **Gospels**: Jesus as fulfillment of hope; resurrection as hope's foundation
- **Paul**: Hope grounded in Christ's resurrection and return
- **Revelation**: Ultimate hope of new heaven and earth

### Related Themes
- Faith & Trust
- God's Promises
- Eternal Life
- Future Kingdom

### Modern Application
- Hope during depression or despair
- Perspective during temporary suffering
- Waiting for God's timing
- Confident expectation despite circumstances

## LOVE (Amor) — Agape, Philos, Storge

### Definition
God's self-giving love; covenant commitment to beloved; extending love to others.

### Types of Love
1. **Agape** (ἀγάπη) — God's divine, self-sacrificing love
2. **Philos** (φιλέω) — Affectionate, friendship love
3. **Storge** (στοργή) — Family/parental love (rarely used in NT)
4. **Eros** (ἔρως) — Romantic/sexual love (avoided in NT theology)

### Key Passages
- **1 Corinthians 13** — "Love is patient, love is kind..."
- **John 3:16** — "God so loved the world..."
- **John 13:34-35** — "Love one another as I have loved you"
- **1 John 4:7-8** — "God is love"
- **Romans 13:8-10** — Love fulfills the law

### Theological Development
- **OT**: God's covenant love (hesed/חסד)
- **Jesus**: Radical redefining of love (love enemies, Sermon on Mount)
- **John**: Love as central theological concept (John 13:34, 1 John)
- **Paul**: Love as supreme fruit of Spirit, fulfillment of law

### Related Themes
- Grace & Mercy
- Forgiveness
- Community & Fellowship
- Sacrifice & Service

### Modern Application
- How we treat others (especially enemies/difficult people)
- Receiving God's love in personal struggle
- Loving sacrificially without keeping score
- Community expression of Christ's love

## GRACE & MERCY (Graça & Misericórdia)

### Definition
Grace: God's unmerited favor and empowerment
Mercy: God's compassionate withholding of judgment

### Key Passages
- **Ephesians 2:8-9** — "By grace you have been saved through faith"
- **Romans 3:21-26** — Righteousness through grace
- **Titus 2:11-14** — Grace teaches us to live godly
- **Psalm 86:15** — God full of mercy and compassion
- **Luke 6:36** — "Be merciful, even as your Father is merciful"

### Theological Development
- **OT**: God's mercy despite disobedience (Exodus 34:6)
- **Paul**: Grace covers sin; empowers righteousness
- **James**: Mercy triumphs over judgment (James 2:13)
- **Hebrews**: God's throne of grace available always (Hebrews 4:16)

### Related Themes
- Forgiveness
- Salvation
- Righteousness
- Redemption

### Modern Application
- Receiving grace when you feel like you've failed
- Extending mercy to those who've wronged you
- Allowing grace to change life patterns
- Offering grace to struggling friends

## JUSTICE & RIGHTEOUSNESS (Justiça & Retidão)

### Definition
Justice: God's fair judgment and establishment of right order
Righteousness: Living in alignment with God's character and will

### Key Passages
- **Amos 5:24** — "Let justice roll down like waters"
- **Proverbs 31:8-9** — Speak up for those who cannot speak
- **Isaiah 1:17** — Learn to do good, seek justice
- **Matthew 23:23** — Justice, mercy, and faith—weightier matters
- **1 John 1:9** — God just to forgive and cleanse

### Theological Development
- **OT Prophets**: God's anger at social injustice, call for righteousness
- **Jesus**: Justice applied to powerless (beatitudes), judgment of unjust systems
- **Paul**: Righteousness from faith; justice in relationships
- **Revelation**: Final justice established in new creation

### Related Themes
- Love & Compassion
- Stewardship & Responsibility
- God's Character
- Kingdom of God

### Modern Application
- Speaking against injustice/oppression
- Using resources to help vulnerable
- Personal integrity and honesty
- Systemic change motivated by biblical justice

## STEWARDSHIP & RESPONSIBILITY (Mordomia & Responsabilidade)

### Definition
Careful management of God's gifts (time, money, talents, relationships, creation) as accountable to God.

### Key Passages
- **1 Peter 4:10** — Stewards of God's grace in various forms
- **Matthew 25:14-30** — Parable of talents
- **Luke 12:48** — "Everyone to whom much has been given, much will be required"
- **Genesis 2:15** — "Tend and keep the garden"
- **Proverbs 27:12** — Prudent see danger, take refuge

### Theological Development
- **OT**: Dominion over creation with responsibility
- **Jesus**: Rich young ruler; parable of stewards; judgment based on stewardship
- **Paul**: Stewards of gospel message; financial responsibility
- **Letters**: Responsible use of gifts for body of Christ

### Related Themes
- Justice & Righteousness
- Wisdom & Understanding
- Community & Fellowship
- God's Ownership

### Modern Application
- Managing finances faithfully
- How we spend time (secular and spiritual)
- Environmental responsibility
- Using gifts to serve others
- Accountability for opportunities given

## WISDOM & UNDERSTANDING (Sabedoria & Entendimento)

### Definition
Wisdom: Insight to apply knowledge rightly to life; fear of God is beginning of wisdom
Understanding: Deep comprehension of truth and its implications

### Key Passages
- **Proverbs 1:7** — "Fear of the Lord is the beginning of wisdom"
- **James 1:5** — Ask God for wisdom
- **Proverbs 3:13-18** — Blessed those who find wisdom
- **1 Corinthians 1:25** — Foolishness of God wiser than human wisdom
- **Psalm 111:10** — Reverence of Lord = beginning of wisdom

### Theological Development
- **Wisdom Literature** (Job, Proverbs, Ecclesiastes): Pursuit of godly wisdom
- **Jesus**: Claims to be wisdom of God; teaches wisdom through parables
- **Paul**: God's wisdom vs. world's wisdom; spiritual discernment
- **James**: Wisdom from above vs. earthly wisdom

### Related Themes
- Knowledge & Truth
- Discernment & Discernment
- Fear of God
- Guidance & Providence

### Modern Application
- Decision-making grounded in God's perspective
- Learning from mistakes (own and others')
- Humility to admit ignorance and seek counsel
- Discernment in information age
- Wise stewardship of resources

## SUFFERING & PERSEVERANCE (Sofrimento & Perseverança)

### Definition
Suffering: Pain and adversity; God's purposes through suffering
Perseverance: Steadfast continuation despite difficulty; endurance

### Key Passages
- **Romans 8:28** — "All things work together for good"
- **Philippians 1:29** — "It has been granted to you not only to believe but also to suffer"
- **Hebrews 12:1-2** — Run race with endurance, fixing eyes on Jesus
- **1 Peter 1:6-7** — Trial of faith more precious than gold
- **James 1:2-4** — Count it all joy when facing trials

### Theological Development
- **OT**: Suffering as consequence of sin; also mystery (Job)
- **Jesus**: Suffering Servant; cross as redemptive suffering
- **Paul**: Rejoice in sufferings; Christ's sufferings continue in us
- **Hebrews & Revelation**: Persecution and suffering in end times
- **1 Peter**: Suffering as participation in Christ's redemption

### Related Themes
- Hope & Redemption
- Trust & Faith
- God's Character
- Victory & Resurrection

### Modern Application
- Finding God's purpose in difficult circumstance
- Encouragement during persecution
- Patience when healing delayed
- Supporting others through suffering
- Growth through adversity

## WORSHIP & PRAISE (Adoração & Louvor)

### Definition
Worship: Honoring God with whole being; recognition of God's worth
Praise: Expression of joy and thanksgiving; proclaiming God's greatness

### Key Passages
- **Psalm 100** — "Enter his gates with thanksgiving"
- **John 4:23-24** — Worship in spirit and truth
- **Romans 12:1** — Present your bodies as sacrifice of worship
- **Philippians 4:4** — "Rejoice in the Lord always"
- **1 Chronicles 16:29** — Ascribe to God glory due his name

### Theological Development
- **OT**: Temple worship; psalms of praise; call to worship
- **Jesus**: Worship shifts from temple to Spirit-centered worship
- **Paul**: Worship as logical response to God's mercy; lifestyle of worship
- **Revelation**: Eternal worship of God and Lamb

### Related Themes
- Love & Devotion
- God's Character
- Community & Fellowship
- Faith & Trust

### Modern Application
- Worship as lifestyle, not just music
- Praising God when circumstances are dark
- Community worship and encouragement
- Personal devotional practices
- Gratitude as spiritual discipline

## COMMUNITY & FELLOWSHIP (Comunidade & Comunhão)

### Definition
Shared life in Christ; believers united as body; mutual care and accountability

### Key Passages
- **Hebrews 10:24-25** — Encourage one another, don't forsake assembling
- **1 John 1:7** — "If we walk in the light as he is in the light, we have fellowship with one another"
- **1 Corinthians 12:12-27** — Body of Christ metaphor
- **Proverbs 27:17** — "Iron sharpens iron"
- **Acts 2:42-47** — Early church community pattern

### Theological Development
- **Jesus**: Called disciples into community; sent them as apostolic team
- **Paul**: Develops body of Christ theology; spiritual gifts for community building
- **Hebrews**: Christian community as mutual encouragement
- **1 John**: Fellowship as vital to spiritual health

### Related Themes
- Love & Service
- Gifts & Calling
- Accountability & Encouragement
- Unity & Diversity

### Modern Application
- Finding authentic community
- Serving within church/small group
- Being present in others' struggles
- Accountability and correction in love
- Diversity within unity

## Theme Study Pattern

For any theme, explore:
1. **Definition** — What does scripture mean by this?
2. **Key Passages** — Primary texts (look up in multiple translations)
3. **Development** — How does theme progress through scripture?
4. **Related Themes** — What connects to this theme?
5. **Modern Application** — How does this apply to my life?
6. **Study Questions** — Reflection prompts
7. **Character Examples** — Who exemplified this in scripture?
8. **Transformation** — How does understanding this change me?
