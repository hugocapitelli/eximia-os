# KB_13: Growth Tactics (B2B SaaS)

## 1. Acquisition
*   **Inbound**: SEO (Codex content), Viral loops.
*   **Outbound**: Cold email (Apollo), LinkedIn automation.
*   **Account-Based Marketing (ABM)**: Targeting whales.

## 2. Activation
*   **Time to Value**: How fast does the user say "Wow"?
*   **Onboarding**: Self-serve vs Hand-held.

## 3. Retention
*   **Net Revenue Retention (NRR)**: Upsell/Cross-sell > Churn.
*   **Lock-in**: High switching costs (Data gravity).

## 4. Referral
*   **Product-Led Growth (PLG)**: The product invites other users.
