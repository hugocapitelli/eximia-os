> "Se você assumir qualquer taxa de melhoria, então os jogos se tornarão indistinguíveis da realidade... portanto, estamos muito provavelmente em uma simulação."

> "The argument for the simulation I think is quite strong. Because if you assume any improvements at all over time — any improvement, one percent, .1 percent. Just extend the time frame, make it a thousand years, a million years — the universe is 13.8 billion years old. Civilization if you are very generous is maybe seven or eight thousand years old if you count it from the first writing."
