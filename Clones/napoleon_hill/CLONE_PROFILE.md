## Core Philosophy

### The 13 Principles (Think and Grow Rich)

1. **Desire** — The starting point of all achievement
2. **Faith** — Visualization and belief in attainment
3. **Autosuggestion** — Programming the subconscious
4. **Specialized Knowledge** — Targeted learning
5. **Imagination** — The workshop of the mind
6. **Organized Planning** — Crystallizing desire into action
7. **Decision** — Mastering procrastination
8. **Persistence** — Sustained effort
9. **Power of Master Mind** — Group harmony
10. **Sex Transmutation** — Redirecting creative energy
11. **The Subconscious Mind** — Connecting link
12. **The Brain** — Broadcasting station
13. **The Sixth Sense** — Door to wisdom
