# Query 01: Biography & Early Life

**Search Date:** 2026-01-23
**Query:** "Napoleon Hill biography early life 1883 childhood education complete history"

---

## Birth & Origins

- **Full Name:** Oliver Napoleon Hill
- **Born:** October 26, 1883
- **Birthplace:** Pound, Wise County, Virginia (one-room cabin in Appalachian region)
- **Died:** November 8, 1970, South Carolina
- **Parents:** James Monroe Hill and Sarah Sylvania (Blair)

## Childhood (1883-1894)

### Early Hardships
- Born into extreme poverty in rural Virginia
- Mother Sarah died in 1892 when Napoleon was only 9 years old
- Father James was ill-equipped as a single parent
- Young Napoleon became increasingly wild and difficult to control

### "Wild Child" Period
- Became enamored with outlaw Jesse James
- Carried a six-shooter on his hip
- Terrorized local citizens in the county
- Described as having an "overactive imagination"

## Transformative Influence: Stepmother Martha (1894)

### The Turning Point
- Father remarried Martha Banner in 1894 (she was a widow)
- Martha saw potential in the troubled boy
- Famous reframe: Told him he wasn't a "bad boy" - just needed to direct his energy toward accomplishing something worthwhile

### The Deal
- Suggested he use his imagination to become a writer
- Promised to buy him a typewriter if he gave up his six-shooter
- Well-educated Martha spent a year personally tutoring him
- Purchased his typewriter in 1895

## Early Writing Career (1896-1901)

### Mountain Reporter
- Started writing for his father's newspaper at age 13 (1896)
- By age 15, was writing articles for many small town newspapers throughout Virginia
- Early writings described as "unrefined and overly imaginative"
- Later admitted to making up stories when the news week was slow

## Education & Early Career (1901-1908)

### Formal Education
- Graduated high school at age 17
- Moved to Tazewell, Virginia to attend business school
- In 1901, began working for coal magnate Rufus Ayres
- Worked for Ayres for 6 months before promotion to clerk in coal mines (Richlands, VA)

### Law School
- Used earnings to enter Georgetown University Law School
- Had to withdraw due to insufficient funds
- His reporter work helped finance partial legal education

### Early Business Ventures
- **Acree-Hill Lumber Company** - First business venture
- Automobile building, driving, and sales
- Multiple failed ventures throughout early career
