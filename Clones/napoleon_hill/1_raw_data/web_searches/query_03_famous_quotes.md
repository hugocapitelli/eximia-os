## On Achievement & Desire

> "The starting point of all achievement is DESIRE. Keep this constantly in mind. Weak desire brings weak results, just as a small fire makes a small amount of heat."

> "Whatever the mind of man can conceive and believe, it can achieve."

> "Set your mind on a definite goal and observe how quickly the world stands aside to let you pass."

> "A goal is a dream with a deadline."

## On Success & Persistence

> "Patience, persistence and perspiration make an unbeatable combination for success."

> "A quitter never wins and a winner never quits."

> "Victory is always possible for the person who refuses to stop fighting."

> "Most great people have attained their greatest success just one step beyond their greatest failure."

> "The majority of men meet with failure because of their lack of persistence in creating new plans to take the place of those which fail."

## On Self-Discipline & Mind Control

> "Self-discipline begins with the mastery of your thoughts. If you don't control what you think, you can't control what you do. Simply, self-discipline enables you to think first and act afterward."

> "If you do not conquer self, you will be conquered by self."

> "You have absolute control over but one thing, and that is your thoughts."

> "The mind is everything. What you think you become."

## On Goals & Action

> "Don't wait. The time will never be just right."

> "Action is the real measure of intelligence."

> "Create a definite plan for carrying out your desire and begin at once, whether you are ready or not, to put this plan into action."

> "First comes thought; then organization of that thought into ideas and plans; then transformation of those plans into reality."

## On Adversity & Growth

> "Every adversity, every failure, every heartache carries with it the seed of an equal or greater benefit."

> "Strength and growth come only through continuous effort and struggle."

> "When defeat comes, accept it as a signal that your plans are not sound, rebuild those plans, and set sail once more toward your coveted goal."

> "Failure is nature's plan to prepare you for great responsibilities."

## On Character & Integrity

> "The man who does more than he is paid for will soon be paid for more than he does."

> "If you cannot do great things, do small things in a great way."

> "Think twice before you speak, because your words and influence will plant the seed of either success or failure in the mind of another."

> "Great achievement is usually born of great sacrifice, and is never the result of selfishness."

## On Dreams & Destiny

> "You are the master of your destiny. You can influence, direct and control your own environment. You can make your life what you want it to be."

> "Cherish your visions and your dreams as they are the children of your soul, the blueprints of your ultimate achievements."

> "Hold a picture of yourself long and steadily enough in your mind's eye, and you will be drawn toward it."

## On Fear & Courage

> "Fear is the tool of a man-made devil. Self-confident faith in oneself is both the man-made weapon which defeats this devil and the man-made tool which builds a triumphant life."

> "Fears are nothing more than a state of mind."

> "The majority of people who fail to accumulate money sufficient for their needs are, generally, easily influenced by the opinions of others."

## On Wealth & Riches

> "Riches begin in the form of thought. The amount is limited only by the person in whose mind the thought is put into motion."

> "More gold has been mined from the thoughts of men than has been taken from the earth."

> "Money without brains is always dangerous."

## On the Master Mind

> "No two minds ever come together without thereby creating a third, invisible intangible force, which may be likened to a third mind."

> "It is literally true that you can succeed best and quickest by helping others to succeed."

## On Definiteness of Purpose

> "There is one quality which one must possess to win, and that is definiteness of purpose, the knowledge of what one wants, and a burning desire to possess it."

> "Until you have formed the habit of looking for the good instead of the bad there is in others, you will be neither successful nor happy."
