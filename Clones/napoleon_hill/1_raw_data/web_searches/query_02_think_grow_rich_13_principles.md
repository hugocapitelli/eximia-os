# Query 02: Think and Grow Rich - 13 Principles

**Search Date:** 2026-01-23
**Query:** "Napoleon Hill Think and Grow Rich 13 principles success philosophy"

---

## About the Book

- **Published:** 1937 (during the Great Depression)
- **Sales:** Over 100 million copies globally
- **Status:** Among the 10 best-selling self-help books of all time
- **Research:** Based on 20+ years of interviews with 500+ successful people

## The 13 Principles of Success

### 1. DESIRE
> "The starting point of all achievement is DESIRE. Keep this constantly in mind. Weak desire brings weak results, just as a small fire makes a small amount of heat."

- The starting point for all achievement
- Must be a burning, obsessive desire
- Not just a wish, but a definite, pulsating desire

### 2. FAITH
- A state of mind that must be learned and cultivated
- Created through repeating affirmations and instructions
- Works through the subconscious via auto-suggestion
- "Faith is the head chemist of the mind"

### 3. AUTOSUGGESTION
- Self-directed communication to channel thoughts
- Bridge between conscious and subconscious mind
- Must be emotionally charged to be effective
- Repetition creates belief

### 4. SPECIALIZED KNOWLEDGE
- General knowledge is not power
- Knowledge must be organized and applied
- Knowing WHERE to find knowledge is key
- Continuous learning is essential

### 5. IMAGINATION
Two types:
- **Synthetic Imagination:** Arranges existing concepts into new combinations
- **Creative Imagination:** The source of hunches and inspiration
- "Ideas are the beginning points of all fortunes"

### 6. ORGANIZED PLANNING
- Crystallize desire into action through planning
- Assemble a "Master Mind" group
- Create practical plans
- Be willing to revise plans when they fail

### 7. DECISION
> "Analysis of several hundred people who had accumulated fortunes well beyond the million dollar mark disclosed that every one of them had the habit of reaching decisions promptly."

- Successful people decide quickly, change slowly
- Failures decide slowly, change quickly
- Follow your own counsel
- Procrastination is the enemy

### 8. PERSISTENCE
> "Persistence is to an individual what carbon is to steel."

- The sustained effort needed to induce faith
- Willpower + desire = irresistible pair
- Most give up just before success
- Can be cultivated as a habit

### 9. POWER OF THE MASTER MIND
- "Coordination of knowledge and effort between two or more people"
- Creates synergy (1+1=3)
- No one achieves great success alone
- Alliance must be harmonious

### 10. THE MYSTERY OF SEX TRANSMUTATION
- Redirecting sexual energy into creative endeavors
- One of the most powerful driving forces
- Can elevate from mediocrity to genius
- Channel energy productively

### 11. THE SUBCONSCIOUS MIND
- Links human mind with Infinite Intelligence
- Works continuously (day and night)
- Responds to emotionalized thoughts
- Must be fed positive thoughts deliberately

### 12. THE BRAIN
- Both a broadcasting and receiving station for thought
- Can pick up thoughts from other minds
- Creative Imagination is the receiving set
- Master Mind is the sending set

### 13. THE SIXTH SENSE
> "Through the aid of the sixth sense, you will be warned of impending dangers in time to avoid them, and notified of opportunities in time to embrace them."

- The culmination of all other principles
- Connection to Infinite Intelligence
- Developed only through meditation and mind development
- The "Temple of Wisdom"

## Core Philosophy

> "Whatever the mind of man can conceive and believe, it can achieve."

The central thesis: **Thoughts are things.** Thinking in a certain way gives you the power to magnetize and attract anything you desire into your life.
