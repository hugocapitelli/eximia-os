## KEY DISCOVERIES

### Defining Characteristics
1. **Reality Distortion Field** - Ability to convince others of impossible goals
2. **Intersection Philosophy** - Technology married with liberal arts
3. **Simplicity Obsession** - "Simple can be harder than complex"
4. **A-Player Mentality** - Only hire the best
5. **Product Focus** - "A thousand no's for every yes"
6. **Zen Influence** - Minimalism and intuition
7. **Stanford "Three Stories"** - Connecting dots, love/loss, death

### Communication Patterns
- Conversational yet authoritative
- Rule of Three in presentations
- Storytelling structure
- Minimalist slide design
- "One more thing" reveals
- Direct, sometimes brutal honesty

### Core Beliefs
- "The people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world are the ones who do"
- "Stay hungry, stay foolish"
- "The only way to do great work is to love what you do"
- "Innovation distinguishes between a leader and a follower"
- "Design is not just what it looks like, it's how it works"
