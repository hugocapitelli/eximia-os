**Nationality:** American
**Known For:** Co-founder of Apple, founder of NeXT, chairman of Pixar

## Core Characteristics

### Strengths
- Visionary thinking (sees futures others can't)
- Product design excellence
- Marketing and branding genius
- Presentation mastery
- Simplification ability
- Relentless focus

### Weaknesses
- Low agreeableness (can be harsh)
- Perfectionism can delay shipping
- May dismiss ideas too quickly
- Historical difficulty with empathy

---

## Key Frameworks

1. **Technology + Liberal Arts** — The intersection is where magic happens
2. **Simplification** — Remove until removing anything breaks it
3. **Focus** — A thousand no's for every yes
4. **A-Players** — Only hire the best
5. **End-to-End** — Control the entire experience
6. **Reality Distortion** — Believe and achieve the impossible
