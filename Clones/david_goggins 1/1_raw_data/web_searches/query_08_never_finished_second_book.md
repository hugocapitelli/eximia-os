# David Goggins - Never Finished (Second Book, 2023)

**Source:** Web Search - "David Goggins second book Never Finished 2023"
**Date:** 2025-12-19

---

## Book Information

**Title:** Never Finished: Unshackle Your Mind and Win the War Within

**Publication:** 2023

**Position:** David Goggins' second book
**Predecessor:** Can't Hurt Me (bestselling memoir)

---

## Sales & Reception

**Sales:** Over 1 million copies sold

**Goggins' Description:** NOT a self-help book but rather "a wake-up call!"

---

## Core Premise

### Evolution from Can't Hurt Me

**Can't Hurt Me:**
- Demonstrated how much untapped ability we have
- Introduction to the power of the mind

**Never Finished:**
- Takes you inside his **Mental Lab**
- Developed philosophy, psychology and strategies
- Learned that what he thought was his limit was only his beginning
- **Key Message:** The quest for greatness is unending

---

## Key Themes

### 1. Mental Grit
Becoming dedicated to discipline and giving your all to all things you commit to.

### 2. The Journey Never Ends
Even after achieving extraordinary success, the work continues. There is no finish line.

### 3. Evolution & Humility
- Goggins' evolution toward humility
- Service to others as ultimate goal

---

## Content & Stories

**Ultra Racing Experiences:**
- Leadville 100
- Moab 240

**Personal Evolution:**
- How he continues to push himself after already achieving "impossible" goals
- Mental strategies developed in his "Mental Lab"

---

## Available Editions

1. **Original Edition**
2. **Curse-Word Free Edition** (for broader audience)

---

## Core Philosophy

### The Mental Lab

Where Goggins developed:
- Philosophy
- Psychology
- Strategies

**Result:** Understanding that perceived limits are just the beginning

### Never Finished Mindset

**Key Insight:** Achievement is not a destination - it's a continuous journey. When you think you're done, you're just getting started.
