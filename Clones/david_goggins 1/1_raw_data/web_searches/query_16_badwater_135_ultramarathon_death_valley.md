# David Goggins - Badwater 135 Ultramarathon (Death Valley)

**Source:** Web Search - "David Goggins Badwater 135 ultramarathon Death Valley performance"
**Date:** 2025-12-19

---

## Race Overview

**Badwater 135:**
- Distance: 135 miles
- Route: Badwater Basin to Whitney Portal
- Elevation Gain: 13,000 feet
- Location: Death Valley, California
- Considered one of the toughest foot races on Earth

---

## Goggins' Badwater Record

**Total Attempts:** 5 times
**Completed:** 3 times (2006, 2007, 2013)
**DNF (Did Not Finish):** 2 times (2008, 2012)

---

## Year-by-Year Performance

### 2006 - First Attempt (Incredible Debut)

**Time:** 30:18:54
**Placement:** 5th place overall

**Context:**
- Incredible result for first attempt
- Weight: 177 lbs (80 kg)
- Inexperienced ultrarunner at the time
- Competing in world-class field

**Significance:** Exceptional performance for a novice in one of world's toughest races.

### 2007 - Best Performance

**Time:** 25:49:40
**Placement:** 3rd place overall

**Improvement:**
- Improved by 4+ hours from previous year
- Nearly made podium

**Winner Context:**
- Course record holder Valmir Nunes finished in 22:51:00
- Goggins finished ~3 hours behind winner

**Achievement:** Career-best Badwater performance.
