## Mental Toughness & Limits

### The 40% Rule
"When you think you've hit your limit, you're only using about 40% of your capability."

### Persistence
"I don't stop when I'm tired, I stop when I'm done."

---

## Obsession & Drive

### Total Commitment
"Be more than motivated, be more than driven, become literally obsessed to the point where people think you're nuts."

---

## Comfort Zones

### Danger of Comfort
"You are in danger of living a life so comfortable and soft that you will die without ever realizing your true potential."

---

## Pain & Growth

### Pain as Gateway
"Pain unlocks a secret doorway in the mind, one that leads to both peak performance, and beautiful silence."

---

## Self-Accountability

### Internal Dialogue
"The most important conversations you'll ever have are the ones you'll have with yourself."

### Daily Reset
"Nobody cares what you did yesterday. What have you done today to better yourself?"

**Source Material:**

## Themes in Goggins' Quotes

1. **Mental vs Physical Limits** - Mind quits before body
2. **Comfort as Enemy** - Soft living prevents growth
3. **Pain as Teacher** - Suffering reveals capability
4. **Self-Accountability** - Only you can push yourself
5. **Obsessive Dedication** - Half-measures don't work
6. **Daily Renewal** - Yesterday doesn't matter
