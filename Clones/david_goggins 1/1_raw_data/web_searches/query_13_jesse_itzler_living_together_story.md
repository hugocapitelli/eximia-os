# David Goggins - Jesse Itzler Living Together Story

**Source:** Web Search - "David Goggins relationships Jesse Itzler living together story"
**Date:** 2025-12-19

---

## The Origin Story

### First Encounter (100-Mile Race)

**When:** 2010
**Where:** 100-mile race

**What Happened:**
- Jesse Itzler was running as part of a relay team
- David Goggins was running the full 100 miles solo
- Goggins weighed about 260 pounds at the time
- He ran without any support staff
- Itzler was speechless witnessing this incredible feat
- Had to meet this man

---

## The Proposition

### Itzler's Goal

**Motivation:** Wanted to become mentally tougher.

**Plan:** Thought the best (or at least most effective) way was by inviting a Navy SEAL to live with him for a month.

### The Approach

**Bold Move:**
- Cold called Goggins
- Flew across the country to meet him in person
- Proposed the idea directly

**The Offer:**
- Pay Goggins to live with his family
- Train him mentally and physically
- Duration: One month

---

## The Agreement

### Goggins' One Rule

**Condition:** Itzler has to do WHATEVER he says, with no exceptions.

**The Deal:**
- Goggins agrees to move in
- Live with Itzler, his wife Sara Blakely (founder of Spanx), and their young child
- Train together for 31 days

---

## The Experience

### Location
**Itzler's Home:** Goggins moved into the family home.

### Intensity
**Training:** Extreme workouts in freezing conditions, demanding training methods.

### Duration
**31 Days:** One month of intense physical and mental training.
