## Early Life and Childhood

**Birth:** February 17, 1975
**Parents:** Trunnis and Jackie Goggins
**Siblings:** Trunnis Jr. (older brother)

### Early Childhood (1981)
- Lived in Williamsville, New York, on Paradise Road
- Father owned Skateland roller-skating rink in East Buffalo, NY
- At age 6, Goggins worked night shifts at Skateland organizing roller skates
- Father was abusive - brutally beat children and their mother regularly

### Escape from Abuse (Age 8)
- Mother left abuser and took David and brother to her parents' house in rural Indiana
- Mother took job at local department store
- Family lived in subsidized housing, struggled financially
- Experienced severe poverty

### Educational Challenges
- Enrolled in third grade with no prior schooling
- Diagnosed with learning disability
- Only African-American kid in school - target for racial prejudice
- Struggled with dyslexia, ADD, and toxic stress from abuse and racism

## Military and Athletic Career

### Navy SEAL Journey
- Lost 106 pounds (48 kg) in 3 months to meet weight requirements
- Graduated from BUD/S training with Class 235 in 2001

### Army Ranger School (2004)
- Only SEAL in his class to graduate
- Earned "Enlisted Honor Man" award
- Received 100% peer evaluation

### Record Achievement
- Successfully broke pull-up record in 2013
- 4,030 pull-ups in 17 hours

### Publication
- Memoir "Can't Hurt Me: Master Your Mind and Defy the Odds"
- New York Times Best Seller
