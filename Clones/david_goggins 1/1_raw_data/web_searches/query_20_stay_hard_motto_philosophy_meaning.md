## Practical Application

### How to Apply "Stay Hard"

**1. Confront Fears**
- Face what scares you
- Run toward challenges
- Don't avoid discomfort
- Use fear as fuel

**2. Set Ambitious Goals**
- Dream big
- Don't settle
- Push beyond comfort
- Raise the bar continually

**3. Maintain Discipline**
- Consistency daily
- No excuses
- Show up regardless
- Build systems, not rely on motivation

**4. Seek New Challenges**
- Never get comfortable
- Continuously grow
- Find harder tasks
- Push limits constantly

## In Context of Goggins' Life

### Who Goggins Is

**Background:**
- American ultramarathon runner
- Ultra-distance cyclist
- Triathlete
- Public speaker
- Author (Can't Hurt Me, Never Finished)
- Retired Navy SEAL (served in Iraq War)

### Why "Stay Hard" Matters to Him

**Personal History:**
- Childhood abuse
- Extreme poverty
- Racism
- Obesity
- Had to "stay hard" to survive and overcome

Philosophy born from lived necessity, not theory.
