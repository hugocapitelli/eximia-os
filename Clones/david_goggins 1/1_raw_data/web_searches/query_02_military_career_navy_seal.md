# David Goggins - Navy SEAL Career & Military Background

**Source:** Web Search - "David Goggins Navy SEAL career military background"
**Date:** 2025-12-19

---

## Overview

David Goggins (born February 17, 1975) is an American motivational speaker, author, and retired United States Navy SEAL.

---

## Military Service Timeline

### Air Force (1994-1999)
- Served as Tactical Air Control Party member
- Initially applied for Air Force Pararescue
- Diagnosed with sickle cell trait during training
- Removed from Pararescue training

### Navy SEAL Training
**Preparation:**
- Lost 106 pounds (48 kg) in 3 months to meet weight requirements
- Joined reserves to begin SEAL training

**Hell Week Challenges:**
- Endured THREE Hell Weeks before graduating
  - **First Hell Week:** Suffered stress fracture and pneumonia
  - **Second Hell Week:** Fractured kneecap, had to stop
  - **Third Hell Week:** Completed successfully two weeks after second attempt
- Graduated from BUD/S with Class 235 in 2001
- Assigned to SEAL Team 5

### Deployments
- Less than one month after 9/11 attacks, deployed to Iraq with SEAL team
- Served in Iraq and Afghanistan during 20-year military career

### Army Ranger School (2004)
- Only SEAL in his class to graduate
- Graduated as "Enlisted Honor Man"
- Received peer evaluation distinction

---

## Unique Achievement

**Triple Crown of Military Training:**

David Goggins is the ONLY member of the U.S. Armed Forces to complete:
1. **SEAL Training** (including two Hell Weeks)
2. **U.S. Army Ranger School** (graduated as Enlisted Honor Man)
3. **Air Force Tactical Air Controller Training**
