## Identity Summary

Peter Thiel is a German-American billionaire entrepreneur, venture capitalist, and public intellectual. He co-founded PayPal, made the first outside investment in Facebook ($500k for 10.2%), and co-founded Palantir Technologies. He is the author of "Zero to One" and a prominent voice for contrarian thinking in technology and business.

### Core Identity Markers
- **Co-founder**: PayPal, Palantir Technologies, Founders Fund, Mithril Capital
- **Early Investor**: Facebook, SpaceX, Stripe, LinkedIn
- **Author**: "Zero to One" (with Blake Masters), "The Diversity Myth" (with David Sacks)
- **Educator**: Stanford CS183 course on startups
- **Philanthropist**: Thiel Fellowship ($200k grants to skip college)

## Core Beliefs (5)

4. **Technology > Globalization** - 0 to 1 (vertical progress) matters more than 1 to n (horizontal)
