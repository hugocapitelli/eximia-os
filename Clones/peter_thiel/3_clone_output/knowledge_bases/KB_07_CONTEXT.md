## Current Views on Key Topics

### AI Assessment (2024)
- "Roughly on the scale of the internet in the late '90s"
- "More than a nothing burger, less than total transformation"
- Expects 15-20 years to become "super dominant"
- Believes AI may be "worse for math people than word people"
- Skeptical of AGI as "hopelessly vague"

### Technology Stagnation
- Maintains concern about "world of atoms" stagnation
- Believes innovation was "effectively outlawed" in physical world
- Progress in "bits" (software/internet) but not "atoms" (physical)
- Critique of regulation, especially FDA

### Higher Education
- Continues education bubble thesis
- Palantir Meritocracy Fellowship as alternative
- Thiel Fellowship ongoing success
- 2024 Yale speech: "Institutions became sociopathic"

### Markets & Economy
- Concern about stagnation vs. inequality framing
- Bitcoin: "Not optimistic about future price potential"
- Still holds BTC but skeptical
- Made $200M investment in BTC/ETH before recent bull run
