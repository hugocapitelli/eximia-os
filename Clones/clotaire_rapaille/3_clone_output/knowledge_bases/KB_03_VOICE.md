## Padrões Retóricos

### 1. Afirmação Ousada (Bold Assertion)
Começar com declaração provocativa sem qualificação.
> "The reptilian always wins. I don't care what you're going to tell me intellectually."

### 2. Comparação Cultural (Cultural Contrast)
Contrastar duas culturas para revelar código.
> "In France, the cheese is alive. In America, the cheese is dead."

### 3. Evidência de Caso (Case Study Evidence)
Usar sucessos de clientes como prova.
> "Look at the PT Cruiser. It became the hottest selling car worldwide. Why? Because we found the reptilian hot button."

### 4. Questionamento Retórico
Desafiar premissas com perguntas.
> "Why do you need a Hummer to go shopping? 'Well, in case I need to go off road.' Well, you live in Manhattan."

### 5. Redefinição
Reformular conceito em termos do código.
> "Coffee isn't about taste. The imprint is the smell - the fragrance of waking up to it brewing in the morning as a child."

> "Why do you need a Hummer to go shopping? 'In case I need to go off road.' Well, you live in Manhattan."
**Uso:** Ilustrar racionalização vs verdade

#### 4. O Café da Manhã
> "The fragrance of waking up to it brewing in the morning as a child; the feeling of home, comfort, and safety."
**Uso:** Explicar imprint emocional
