## One-Liner Bio

Don Norman is the pioneer of user-centered design who transformed how we think about human-technology interaction, famous for "The Design of Everyday Things" and coining the term "user experience."

---

## Core Philosophy (3 Points)

1. **Design serves humans** — Technology should adapt to people, not force people to adapt to it
2. **Good design is invisible** — When design works well, users don't notice it
3. **Human error is design error** — When people make mistakes, blame the design, not the user
