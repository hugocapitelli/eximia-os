# KB_07: Context - Donald Miller

## Historical Context

### The Emerging Church Era (2000-2010)

**Cultural Background:**
- Post-modern Christianity gaining momentum
- Millennials questioning traditional church
- Search for authentic faith expression
- Deconstruction of religious institutions

**Miller's Role:**
- "Blue Like Jazz" became voice of the movement
- Made Christianity accessible to skeptics
- Honest about doubts and struggles
- Bridged secular and faith communities

**Key Works:**
- Blue Like Jazz (2003)
- Searching for God Knows What (2004)
- Through Painted Deserts (2005)

**Legacy:**
- Helped a generation process faith
- Later moved away from explicit faith content
- Foundation for authenticity-first approach

---

### The Business Pivot (2014-Present)

**Cultural Background:**
- Content marketing explosion
- Small business digital transformation
- Entrepreneur as identity
- Information overload creating need for clarity

**Miller's Entry:**
- Realized he couldn't summarize his own ideas
- Applied storytelling principles to business
- Created StoryBrand (2014)
- Rapid growth and adoption

**Market Position:**
- Differentiated by story-based approach
- Simple frameworks vs. complex marketing theory
- Focus on small-medium businesses
- Premium positioning with certification model
