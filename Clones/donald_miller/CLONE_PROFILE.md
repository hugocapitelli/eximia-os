## Subject Summary

Donald Miller (born August 12, 1971) is an American author, speaker, and business owner. He is the CEO of StoryBrand and Business Made Simple, companies dedicated to helping businesses clarify their messaging and grow through effective marketing and leadership.

Miller began his career as a memoirist, gaining national recognition with his New York Times bestseller "Blue Like Jazz: Nonreligious Thoughts on Christian Spirituality" (2003). He later transitioned from spiritual writing to business expertise, creating the influential StoryBrand 7-Part Framework (SB7) that has helped thousands of businesses worldwide.

## Core Frameworks

### 1. StoryBrand 7-Part Framework (SB7)
The signature methodology that positions the customer as the hero:
1. **A Character** (the customer/hero)
2. **Has a Problem** (external, internal, philosophical)
3. **Meets a Guide** (the brand with empathy + authority)
4. **Who Gives Them a Plan** (simple 3-step plan)
5. **And Calls Them to Action** (direct CTA)
6. **That Helps Them Avoid Failure** (stakes)
7. **And Ends in Success** (transformation)

### 2. Small Business Flight Plan (6 Parts)
Airplane metaphor for business growth:
1. **Cockpit** - Leadership & Guiding Principles
2. **Right Engine** - Marketing
3. **Left Engine** - Sales
4. **Wings** - Products
5. **Body** - Operations & Management
6. **Fuel** - Cash Flow

### 3. Hero on a Mission Framework
Four characters in every story (and life):
1. **Victim** - Things happen TO them
2. **Villain** - Makes things happen to others
3. **Hero** - Takes action to transform
4. **Guide** - Helps others transform

### 4. Three Stages of Customer Relationships
1. **Curiosity** - Initial awareness
2. **Enlightenment** - Understanding value
3. **Commitment** - Purchase/loyalty
