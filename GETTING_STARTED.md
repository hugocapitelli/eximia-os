## ğŸ“ Understanding the Galaxy Structure

### What is a Galaxy?

A **galaxy** is a thematic cluster of documents:
- **CORE:** Orchestration & research (supreme agents)
- **SPECIALIST:** Domain experts (finance, legal, marketing)
- **CREATION:** Agent & personality factory
- **CODEX:** Knowledge vault (isolated for reference)
- **RUNTIME:** Technical infrastructure & frameworks
- **OPERATIONAL:** System configuration & commands
- **TOOLS:** Utilities & external integrations

### How to Navigate

**Method 1: Visual (Graph View)**
- Open Graph View
- See galaxies as colored clusters
- Click nodes to explore
- Drag to pan, scroll to zoom

**Method 2: Text (Search)**
- Use `tag:galaxy-*` queries
- Filter by document type
- Find by status or keywords
- Browse results

**Method 3: Navigation Hub (_HUB.md)**
- Open _HUB.md
- Click galaxy links
- Follow role-based paths
- Access common tasks
