## Sobre Aprendizagem

> "Learning is the process whereby knowledge is created through the transformation of experience."
> — *Definicao central de aprendizagem experiencial*

> "Knowledge results from the combination of grasping and transforming experience."
> — *Sobre a natureza dual do conhecimento*

> "Learning, the creation of knowledge and meaning, occurs through the active extension and grounding of ideas and experiences in the external world and through internal reflection about the attributes of these experiences and ideas."
> — *Sobre o processo de aprendizagem*

> "We have cast our lot with learning, and learning will pull us through."
> — *Sobre a necessidade de aprendizagem para sobrevivencia*

> "Pleasure is the state of being brought about by what you learn. Learning is the process of entering into the experience of this kind of pleasure. No pleasure, no learning. No learning, no pleasure."
> — *Wang Ken, citado por Kolb na introducao*

## Sobre Experiencia

> "Everything begins and ends in the continuous flux and flow of experience."
> — *Influencia de William James*

> "Experience is already overlaid and saturated with the products of the reflection of past generations and by-gone ages. It is filled with interpretations, classifications, due to sophisticated thought, which have become incorporated into what seems to be fresh naive empirical material."
> — *John Dewey, citado sobre experiencia cultural*

> "Truth is not manifest in experience; it must be inferred by a process of learning that questions preconceptions of direct experience, tempers the vividness and emotion of experience with critical reflection, and extracts the correct lessons from the consequences of action."
> — *Sobre a necessidade de processar experiencia*

> "The lessons derived from experiential learning are rife with unjustified conclusions, superstitious associations, misleading correlations, tautological generalizations, and systematic biases."
> — *James March, sobre os riscos da experiencia nao processada*

## Sobre Educacao

> — *John Dewey sobre experiencia e educacao*

> "To imposition from above is opposed expression and cultivation of individuality; to external discipline is opposed free activity; to learning from texts and teachers, learning through experience."
> — *Dewey sobre educacao progressista*

> "Learning to adapt to new 'rules of the game' is becoming as critical as performing well under the old rules."
> — *Sobre mudanca e adaptacao*

> "Experiential learning theory offers the foundation for an approach to education and learning as a lifelong process that is soundly based in intellectual traditions of social psychology, philosophy, and cognitive psychology."
> — *Fundamentacao teorica*

## Sobre Aprendizagem ao Longo da Vida

> "Lifelong learning is the norm, not the exception, in a changing world."
> — *Normalizacao da aprendizagem continua*

> "Between 80 and 90 percent of the adult population will carry out at least one learning project this year, and the typical adult will spend 500 hours during the year learning new things."
> — *Tough, citado sobre aprendizagem adulta*

> "The future learning society represents a personal challenge for millions of adults who find learning is no longer 'for kids' but a central lifelong task essential for personal development and career success."
> — *Sobre a sociedade de aprendizagem*

> "Learning is an increasing occupation for us all; for in every aspect of our life and work, to stay abreast of events and to keep our skills up to the 'state of the art' requires more and more of our time and energy."
> — *Aprendizagem como ocupacao*
