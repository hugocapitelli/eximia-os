## 3. Cap Table Management

### Dilution Math (YC, 2020)
```
New Ownership = Investment / Post-Money Valuation
Existing Dilution = 1 - (Pre-Money / Post-Money)
```

### Option Pool
Segundo Y Combinator (SAFE Primer, 2020):
- Standard: 10-15% pre-money
- Impact: Reduces founder ownership, not investor

### Pro-Rata Rights
- Definition: Right to invest in future rounds to maintain %
- Standard: Major investors (>5% ownership)
- Brasil: Comum em Series A+
