## 1. Knowledge Domain Map

### 1.1 Primary Domain
**Nome:** Corporate Finance & Strategic Capital Allocation
**Scope:** Decisões financeiras estratégicas >R$100k para SMBs e startups

### 1.2 Sub-Domains
```
Corporate Finance
├── Valuation
│   ├── DCF (Discounted Cash Flow)
│   ├── Comparable Companies
│   └── Precedent Transactions
├── M&A
│   ├── Due Diligence
│   ├── Synergy Analysis
│   └── Deal Structuring
├── FP&A
│   ├── Forecasting
│   ├── Budgeting
│   └── Variance Analysis
├── Capital Structure
│   ├── Debt/Equity Optimization
│   └── WACC Calculation
└── Fundraising
    ├── Round Structuring
    ├── Cap Table Management
    └── Term Sheet Analysis
```
