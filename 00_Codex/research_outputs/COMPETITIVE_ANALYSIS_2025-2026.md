## COMPETITOR LANDSCAPE

### Direct Competitors (Brazil)

#### 1. **MOBILLS** (Acquired by Nubank 2022)
- **FocUS:** Personal finance + budget
- **Users:** ~5M+ (within Nubank ecosystem)
- **Features:** Expense tracking, budgeting, investment tracking (basic)
- **Monetization:** Freemium + premium features
- **Gaps:** Limited Pix integration, weak on subscriptions, basic AI

#### 2. **ORGANIZZE** (Still Independent)
- **Focus:** Expense tracking + personal finance
- **Users:** ~2M+
- **Features:** Categories, budgets, reports, invoice generation
- **Monetization:** Freemium (R$ 20/m√™s premium)
- **Gaps:** No investment tracking, weak fraud detection, basic business features

#### 3. **NUBANK** (Neo-bank with fintech features)
- **Focus:** Bank accounts + investments + personal finance
- **Users:** 100M+ (LAC-wide)
- **Features:** Expense tracking, investment (B3 stocks), credit score (via Open Finance), **Pix management**, notifications, fraud detection
- **Monetization:** Banking services + credit products
- **Strengths:** Scale, brand, brand loyalty, regulatory licenses
- **Gaps:** Less focused on detailed budgeting, business finance is weak, no micro-savings

#### 4. **INTER** (Digital Bank)
- **Focus:** Banking + investments + payments
- **Users:** 10M+
- **Features:** Pix, investments, expense tracking, credit products
- **Gaps:** Less gamified, UX weaker than Nubank

#### 5. **PICPAY** (Payment app with finance)
- **Focus:** Payments + micro-investments
- **Users:** 40M+
- **Features:** Pix, round-up investing (Raio), rewards
- **Gaps:** Limited budgeting, expense tracking basic
